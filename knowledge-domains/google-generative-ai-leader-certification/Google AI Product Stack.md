- enterprise AI platform：[[Vertex AI]]
- model catalog：[[Model Garden]]
- multimodal model family：[[Gemini]]
- rapid prototyping：[[Google AI Studio]]
- enterprise app building：[[Vertex AI Studio]]
- productivity assistant：[[Gemini for Workspace]]
- document analysis assistant：[[NotebookLM]]
- broader Google Cloud AI solutions：[[Google Cloud GenAI Offerings]]
