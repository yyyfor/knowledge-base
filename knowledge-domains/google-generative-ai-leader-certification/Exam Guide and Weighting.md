# Exam Guide and Weighting

根据官方 study guide，考试重点并不是平均分布，而是更偏业务落地、产品理解和企业采用。

主要权重：

- Fundamentals of generative AI: 30%
- Google Cloud generative AI offerings: 20%
- Techniques to improve generative AI model output: 20%
- Business strategies for a successful generative AI solution: 30%

题型特点：

- multiple choice
- scenario-based questions
- best-solution selection

不强调：

- coding
- deep math
- algorithm derivation
