# Quant Programmer Learning Sequence

如果从零到一规划学习路径，建议按以下顺序推进。

1. [[Programming Languages for Quants]]
2. [[Data Structures and Algorithms for Quants]]
3. [[Probability and Statistics for Quants]]
4. [[Time Series and Data Engineering]]
5. [[Derivatives for Quant Developers]]
6. [[Risk and Valuation Basics]]
7. [[Numerical Methods]]
8. [[Software Engineering for Quants]]
9. [[Distributed Systems for Finance]]
10. [[Quant Programmer Interview Preparation]]

详细解释：

先把通用编程和数学打牢，再逐步补金融和系统，不要一开始就陷入复杂模型细节。能把“数据 -> 模型 -> 系统 -> 风险解释”打通，才是真正有竞争力的量化程序员。
