# Quant Programmer Learning Sequence

如果从零到一规划学习路径，建议按以下顺序推进。

如果想先用“交易视角”建立全局框架，可以先快速读一遍 [[Quant Trading Foundations from Prediction Markets]]，再按下面顺序系统补课。

1. [[Programming Languages for Quants]]
2. [[Probability and Statistics for Quants]]
3. [[Data Structures and Algorithms for Quants]]
4. [[Linear Algebra for Quants]]
5. [[Calculus and Optimization]]
6. [[Time Series and Data Engineering]]
7. [[Backtesting and Research Workflow]]
8. [[Market Microstructure]]
9. [[Derivatives for Quant Developers]]
10. [[Risk and Valuation Basics]]
11. [[Numerical Methods]]
12. [[Software Engineering for Quants]]
13. [[Distributed Systems for Finance]]
14. [[Model Validation and Controls]]
15. [[Quant Programmer Interview Preparation]]

详细解释：

先把通用编程和数学打牢，再尽早把“概率 -> 统计 -> 研究流程 -> 执行机制 -> 定价 -> 系统”串起来，不要一开始就只盯着某个复杂模型。能把“数据 -> 模型 -> 系统 -> 风险解释”打通，才是真正有竞争力的量化程序员。
