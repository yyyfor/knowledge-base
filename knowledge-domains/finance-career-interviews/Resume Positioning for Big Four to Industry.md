# Resume Positioning for Big Four to Industry

四大转甲方或互联网公司，简历最大的问题通常不是经历不够，而是语言没有翻译。

你不能只写“参与某某审计项目、负责某某科目、完成底稿”。企业面试官想看的是：你能解决什么业务问题、降低什么风险、提升什么效率、推动什么结果。

## Positioning Shift

从四大语言转成企业语言：

- 审计科目 -> 业务流程和风险点。
- 底稿 -> 数据验证和证据链。
- 调整分录 -> 财务准确性和管理决策影响。
- 内控测试 -> 流程治理和风险预防。
- 客户沟通 -> stakeholder management。
- 项目管理 -> deadline、质量、团队协作。
- 抽样测试 -> 数据分析和异常识别。

## Resume Bullet Formula

建议用：

Action + Business Context + Complexity + Impact

不要写：

- Responsible for revenue audit.
- Assisted in internal control testing.
- Prepared audit working papers.

更好的写法：

- Led revenue audit workstream for a multi-entity client, reconciled order, billing, and GL data, and identified process gaps that improved revenue cut-off accuracy.
- Performed end-to-end walkthrough of procurement-to-pay process, identified approval and vendor master data control gaps, and aligned remediation actions with finance and operations teams.
- Managed junior team members across multiple audit areas, reviewed workpapers, resolved client data issues, and ensured timely delivery under compressed reporting timeline.

## 不同岗位的定位

### 财务 / FP&A

强调：

- revenue / cost / margin analysis
- budgeting / forecasting
- management reporting
- business performance insight
- financial close and reporting quality
- cross-functional communication

四大经历怎么翻译：

- 审计收入 -> 理解收入确认、订单到回款流程、收入数据口径。
- 审计费用 -> 理解费用控制、预算偏差、供应商和审批。
- 审计存货/成本 -> 理解成本归集、毛利、供应链和库存风险。

### 内审 / 内控

强调：

- risk assessment
- process walkthrough
- control design and testing
- remediation tracking
- fraud risk
- data analytics
- management reporting

四大经历怎么翻译：

- 控制测试 -> 内控设计和有效性评估。
- audit finding -> 风险识别和整改推动。
- 客户沟通 -> 业务 stakeholder alignment。

### 互联网业务财务

强调：

- business model understanding
- KPI analysis
- system and data awareness
- fast iteration
- ROI and resource allocation
- finance process design for new business

四大经历怎么翻译：

- 多行业项目 -> 快速理解新业务。
- 系统数据核对 -> 数据口径和系统链路意识。
- 风险发现 -> 新业务上线前的流程和控制设计。

## 面试自我介绍模板

中文版本：

我过去主要在四大负责 [行业/客户类型] 的审计和内控相关项目，覆盖过 [收入/成本/费用/存货/资金/系统流程] 等模块。相比单纯做底稿，我更关注业务流程背后的风险和数据口径，比如 [举一个例子]。这也是我现在想转到甲方/互联网财务或内审岗位的原因：希望从项目制的风险识别，进一步进入长期的业务支持、流程优化和机制建设。

英文版本：

My background is in Big Four audit and internal control projects, mainly covering areas such as revenue, cost, expenses, inventory, cash, and business process controls. What I found most valuable was not just completing audit procedures, but understanding how business processes, system data, and financial reporting connect. That is why I am now looking to move into an industry role where I can contribute more directly to business finance, risk management, process improvement, and long-term control design.

## 简历常见问题

- 只写职责，不写结果。
- 全是审计术语，业务方看不懂。
- 没有体现沟通和推动。
- 没有体现数据分析或系统意识。
- 没有根据目标岗位调整重点。
